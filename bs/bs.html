How to play that Game

With the blue Joystick can you move your Brawler in any direction. With the Red Joystick can you attack other Brawlers with aiming if you push the Joystick in a direction. Tap on the Joystick to Auto Aim enemys. Every Brawler has 3 munition somthimes 4 munition that reloads in a time. If you shoot an enemy, you load your Ultimate Attack on. To use it you have to Tap or Aim with pushing the Button in the center with the yellow Jostick. To use the green Gadget you need to press it to activate the capability.
