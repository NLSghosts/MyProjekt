With the blue Joystick can you move your Brawler in any direction. With the Red Joystick can you attack other Brawlers with aiming if you push the Joystick in a direction.     Coins

    With Coins you can upgrade your Brawler and buy Coin Skins with Coins for every brawler. It has two version of Coin Skins the first is Silver and the second is Gold, you have to buy first the Silver Skin and thereafter the Gold version Skin. You can find Coins on the Trophy Road, in the Brawl Pass, from Star Drops as rewards from quests and Challanges.

    Bling

    Bling is used to buy Skins, Pins, Sprays and Profil Icons. You can collect Bling on the Brawl Pass, Trophy Reset, Ranked, Star Drops and from Brawler Rank Ups.

    Gems

    Gems are used to buy Brawlers, Skins and other things from the Shop. You get Gems from Brawl Pass rewards or purchase from the Shop with real money.
