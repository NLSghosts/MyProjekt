    • Chest

    In the Chest you gain Cards for Uprading a Brawler, Coins and Gems. Clash Royal has very much different Chests in the game you can get this Chests by Playing the normal game, Trophy Road, Shop and Events.
    to open these chests you have a Timer to unlock the chest that means you need to wait until the timer has run out so you can open the chest. The better the chest, the longer you have to wait for the timer to be finished (Only Normal round of Clash Royal).

    Pass Royal

    Pass Royal is a reward path who you can unlock things like coins,chests,gems,Joker,Pin and many more. But to unlock it you have to earn Crowns that you can get by playing a normal game of Clash Royal by destroying their towers in the game to go further in the Pass Royal path
